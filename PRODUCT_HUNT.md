### Tagline (120 chars)

### Description (Up to 260 words)

**The Problem:**
Your brain wasn't built to be a task manager. Every "I should..." thought takes up mental RAM. By noon, you're overwhelmed with a dozen mental tabs open, unsure what actually matters.

**The Solution:**
MindClear gives you cognitive relief through a simple 3-step process:

**1. Dump Everything**
Text or voice - pour out whatever's weighing on your mind. No organizing, no filtering. Just pure brain dump.

**2. AI Processes**
Claude AI reads your chaos and extracts what matters: tasks, commitments, deadlines. It separates actionable items from emotional noise (and acknowledges your stress).

**3. Get Your 3 Items**
Every morning, see exactly 3 focus items. Not 20. Not 50. Three. Done, park, or drop each one.

**Key Features:**
- Voice & text capture (hold-to-record makes it effortless)
- Context-aware AI (remembers what you already captured, no duplicates)
- Emotional acknowledgment (validates when you're venting vs. planning)
- Smart deadline detection (extracts "tomorrow" ‚Üí actual dates)
- Daily 3-item limit (forces prioritization, prevents overwhelm)

**Built for:**
People drowning in mental tasks. Overwhelmed knowledge workers. Anyone who's ever thought "I have so much to do I don't know where to start."

**Tech:**
Next.js, Supabase, Claude AI (Anthropic), Deepgram voice transcription, beautiful glassmorphism UI.

Free to start. No credit card required.

## üí° FAQ Preparation

**Q: How is this different from todo apps?**
A: Todo apps make you organize. MindClear makes you dump. The AI does the organizing.

**Q: What if I have more than 3 things to do?**
A: You do. But you can only truly focus on 3 today. MindClear forces prioritization.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes. End-to-end encrypted, stored in Supabase. We don't train on your data.

**Q: How much does AI processing cost you?**
A: ~$0.02 per dump. Sustainable at scale with monetization.

**Q: Will there be a mobile app?**
